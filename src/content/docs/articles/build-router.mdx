By supporting both EVM and non-EVM chains, Router Protocol ensures that developers can build dApps that leverage the strengths of multiple blockchains, without being locked into a single ecosystem.

Another important aspect of Router Protocol is its focus on security. Cross-chain interactions inherently involve a higher level of risk, as they require trust between different blockchain networks. To mitigate this risk, Router Protocol employs a number of security measures, including cryptographic proofs and multi-signature mechanisms, to ensure that cross-chain transactions are secure and reliable.
